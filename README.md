## Datasets

- S/R: Synthetic (S) or Real (R) or Both (B)
- C/D: Color (RGB) or Depth (D)
- Obj: Interaction with objects or not
- #J:  No. of joints or Mesh (M)
- V: view (3rd or egocentric)
- #S: No. of subjects
- #F: No. of frames (train/test)
- Mesh: Mesh annotations
